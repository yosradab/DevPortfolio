I am an Electrical Engineer with 3 years of experience in the aerospace and automotive industries. I specialize in 3D design, electrical harness routing, and embedded systems integration. Skilled in Siemens NX, CATIA V5, and E3 Zuken, I create efficient, safety-compliant designs that meet the highest industry standards.

My experience spans the full product lifecycle — from initial concept routing to production documentation — using PLM systems to ensure seamless data management. I thrive in cross-functional, international environments and bring a detail-oriented approach to every project. Je suis Ingénieur Électricien avec 3 ans d'expérience dans les industries aérospatiale et automobile.
